About the Event

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 gathers students, developers, and community innovators to build prototypes addressing urban climate challenges. Through workshops and mentoring, participants will transform data into solutions that cool cities and prevent floods.

  • Build prototypes tackling heat and flood risks
  • Collaborate across disciplines
  • Develop real-world impact solutions
